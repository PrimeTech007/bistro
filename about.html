Meet Chef Miko Dy

The Heart Behind Bistro Lokal

"With over 15 years of experience in the Filipino culinary scene, Chef Miko Dy brings artistry, passion, and deep-rooted tradition to every plate."

Bistro Lokal is Baguio City's first restaurant to offer a full Menu Dégustation — a 10 to 12-course tasting experience that celebrates Filipino cuisine at its finest. As Chef Miko says: "This is not just a business. It is a passion restaurant. We want to show people what food is truly all about."

Every dish that leaves our kitchen is a testament to Chef Miko's dedication to elevating Filipino flavors while honoring their authentic roots.
